Destinations in Sri Lanka

Colombo

Sri Lanka’s bustling capital blends modern city life with colonial charm. Explore lively markets, visit the Gangaramaya Temple, stroll along Galle Face Green, and enjoy the city’s rich culinary scene.

Kandy

Home to the famous Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic, Kandy is surrounded by lush hills and a serene lake. It’s the perfect base to experience traditional Kandyan dance performances and visit the Royal Botanical Gardens.

Nuwara Eliya

Famous for its cool climate and tea plantations, Nuwara Eliya offers lush gardens, scenic train rides, and charming colonial-era architecture. Don’t miss a visit to Gregory Lake and Hakgala Botanical Garden.

Ella

A favorite among nature lovers, Ella boasts stunning views, waterfalls, and hiking trails. Popular spots include Nine Arch Bridge, Ella Rock, and Little Adam’s Peak.
